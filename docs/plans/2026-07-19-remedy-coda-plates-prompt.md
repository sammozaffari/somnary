Companion set to the hero plates (`docs/plans/2026-07-16-remedy-hero-plates-prompt.md`).
The melatonin coda was approved as north star 2026-07-19 with two amendments, folded in
below (density ceiling; strict calm-top-third). Outputs land beside the hero masters in the

**Somnary — remedy coda plates: 31 full-bleed linocut FOOTER images**

You previously generated the 31 approved hero plates for Somnary's remedy pages
(`somnary-remedy-heroes/{slug}.png`). Now generate their companions: a **footer plate per
remedy** — the closing image at the bottom of each page. Where the hero opens the remedy's
world at dusk, the footer is its **coda: the same world, an hour later and closer to the
ground, everything settled toward sleep.**

**Same printmaker, same world.** Every footer must read as cut by the same hand as its hero,
from the same block series:

- Match each footer's **palette and carving grain EXACTLY to its own hero plate** — sample
  the inks from `{slug}.png` in the hero set; introduce no new colors. Same reduction-print
  vocabulary: carved hatching, ink texture, paper grain, slight misregistration. No
  photorealism, no digital gradients.
- **Density ceiling (north-star amendment):** the approved melatonin coda's wave line-work is
  the FINE EDGE of the family — it survives only because it is high-contrast on near-black.
  Footers display as narrow horizontal slices; hold that density as the ceiling, never push
  past it.
- Mood shift, not scene change: **quieter, darker or stiller than the hero.** Blooms closed,
  objects set down, water gone to glass, embers low. Continuation, not repetition — never
  reuse the hero's focal subject at the same angle.
- Landscape **2880×1620 (16:9), PNG, sRGB**, chunky enough to survive a 50% crop.
- **Composition INVERTED from the hero — strictly:** the **TOP third calm and tonally even**
  (sky, water, ground, or paper; the page dissolves into the plate from above — this rule is
  what makes the short-band crop work on every remedy). Rich detail in the lower two-thirds
  and edges. Focal subject within the central 60% of width. **No icon, emblem, badge, or
  placeholder in the image.**

**Hard constraints (unchanged from the hero brief):** no text, letters, or numerals; no
people (silhouettes included); no pills, capsules, bottles, jars, or packaging (traditional
objects fine where noted); vermilion/scarlet `#E34234` never dominant; brand oxblood
`#7E1F2B` never dominant; palettes botanical, never evaluative.

**Process:** the **melatonin coda is APPROVED as north star** (dark sea below the hero's
sky, moon's path broken on the water, amber horizon reduced to a last thread — the moon
itself never shown). Batch the remaining 30 against it and deliver a contact sheet. Name
files `{slug}.png` in `somnary-remedy-footers/`.
