When to see a doctor

Supplements are for mild, short-term sleeplessness. These signs may need medical care before another product.

Apnea signsLoud snoring, gasping, or pauses in breathing during sleep. Chronic insomniaTrouble sleeping 3 nights per week for 3 months. Restless legsUncomfortable urge to move legs, worse at night. Daytime sleep attacksFalling asleep during the day or while driving. Mood or self-harmDepression, hopelessness, or thoughts of self-harm. What to bring to an appointment

BringWhy it matters
Sleep diaryShows timing, awakenings, and patterns over one to two weeks.
Everything you takeSupplements, OTC products, prescriptions, alcohol, and cannabis can interact.
Partner observationsSnoring, breathing pauses, leg movement, or acting out dreams can change the diagnosis.
Safety-critical tasksDriving, shift work, and machinery make next-day drowsiness more serious.

The honest boundary

Somnary can tell you what evidence and labels show. It cannot diagnose, personalize a dose, or rule out a sleep disorder.

Safety before supplement optimization
This is not medical advice. Seek urgent help for self-harm, overdose, breathing trouble, or an unrousable person.
