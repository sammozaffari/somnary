# 90-Day Action Plan And Implementation Advice

## North Star

Increase the number of users who make a better, safer sleep-remedy decision because Somnary clarified evidence, label reality, or a clinician boundary.

## Key Metrics

Primary:

- remedy checks completed;
- label checks completed;
- newsletter signup rate;
- return visits after first remedy page;
- source/citation engagement;
- safety-page click-through.

Quality:

- correction submissions resolved;
- unsupported claim rate;
- AI answer citation accuracy;
- pages with review date;
- pages with “best for / not for / safety” module.

## First 30 Days

### IA And Homepage

Actions:

- rewrite homepage first screen around “Check a sleep remedy before you take it”;
- add decision tiles for melatonin, sleep blends, falling asleep, waking at night, medication safety, children;
- add “how to read a grade” in plain English;
- add Start Here page.

Acceptance criteria:

- a first-time user understands what Somnary does in under 8 seconds;
- the homepage offers at least three clear decision routes;
- the no-affiliate/no-brand-money promise remains visible.

### Content

Actions:

- build Melatonin Decision Hub;
- build Sleep Blend Label Checker MVP;
- build Safety Hub outline;
- start Evidence Watch page.

### Growth

Actions:

- launch newsletter signup with “Sleep Claims Watch” promise;
- publish 4 reusable short-form scripts;
- create a claim-submission form.

## Days 31-60

### Audience Pages

Build:

- Before giving a child melatonin;
- Before using sleep supplements with medications;
- Jet lag and shift work;
- Anxiety-driven insomnia;
- Older adults and sleep-aid risk.

### Product MVPs

Build:

- static label-checker flow;
- remedy comparison table by outcome;
- printable clinician/pharmacist question checklist.

### Channel System

Actions:

- publish weekly newsletter;
- start YouTube with 4 episodes;
- post 3 TikToks/Reels per week using claim-check format;
- post 2 X.com threads per week tied to sources.

## Days 61-90

### AI Prototype

Actions:

- build scoped page assistant;
- test hallucination and refusal prompts;
- add AI policy page;
- route risk questions to safety pages;
- log unanswered questions as content opportunities.

### Partnerships

Actions:

- recruit 2 pharmacist reviewers;
- recruit 1 CBT-I clinician advisor;
- pitch 5 newsletter swaps;
- pitch consumer-protection writers on sleep-label teardowns.

### Paid Tests

Actions:

- test search ads for melatonin and sleep blends;
- test paid social for label-checker lead magnet;
- measure newsletter signups and landing-page scroll depth.

## Backlog

- supplement QA scoring model;
- public evidence API;
- browser extension;
- PDF handouts;
- clinician portal;
- community claim voting;
- product-label image parsing;
- citation export;
- corrections dashboard.

## Risks To Watch

- AI drifting into medical advice;
- affiliate temptation weakening the brand;
- community submissions becoming anecdotal treatment advice;
- creator collaborations needing disclosures;
- ad copy making claims the pages do not substantiate;
- too much content breadth before the melatonin/sleep-blend wedge is owned.
