  melatonin supplements (16 brands, purchased in Guelph ON) analyzed by UPLC; melatonin content was outside
  ±10% of label in >71% of products (range 83% below to 478% above), lot-to-lot variability up to 465%;
  serotonin detected in 26%. **The authors did not disclose the brands tested**, so no attribution to CVS is
  possible. General category context only. https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27855744/

  products; 88% inaccurately labeled (74%–347% of label); one contained CBD and no melatonin. Gummy-segment
