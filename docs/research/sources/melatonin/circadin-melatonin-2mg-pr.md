## Assays / content analyses (→ source_assays)

This is the strong column for a registered medicine. A supplement dossier looks
for third-party HPLC assays of the specific bottle because supplement content is
not regulator-guaranteed. Circadin's melatonin content is instead guaranteed by
GMP manufacture and the quality module of its regulatory dossier (assessed in
the EPAR and AusPAR above); there is therefore no external "does the bottle
contain what the label says" assay problem to resolve for this product.
