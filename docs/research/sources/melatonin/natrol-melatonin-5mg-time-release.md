### Manufacturer / parent (note: task premise partially corrected)
The task brief listed the parent as "Aurobindo/Jarrow." The public record shows a
handoff:
- Natrol was owned by **Aurobindo Pharma** until **2021**, when Aurobindo agreed
  to sell Natrol to an affiliate of **New Mountain Capital**
  (https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20201025005024/en/New-Mountain-Capital-to-Acquire-Natrol-from-Aurobindo-Pharma).
- New Mountain then combined Natrol with **Jarrow Formulas** under a new company,
  **Vytalogy Wellness**, announced **2022-01-11**
  (https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220111005474/en/Natrol-and-Jarrow-Formulas-Come-Together-Under-New-Company-as-Vytalogy-Wellness).
- So the **current** parent is Vytalogy Wellness (New Mountain Capital); Aurobindo
  is the **former** parent. The Natrol product/safety pages themselves reference
  "Vytalogy Wellness" and "Natrol and Jarrow"
  (https://www.natrol.com/pages/product-safety-quality, fetched 2026-07-19).
