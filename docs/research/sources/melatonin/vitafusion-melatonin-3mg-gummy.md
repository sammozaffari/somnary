- **event_type**: recall (voluntary, nationwide)
- **agency**: FDA (company-announced, FDA-posted); recall subsequently terminated
- **event_date**: April 2021 (lots manufactured Oct 29–Nov 3, 2020; distributed
  Nov 13, 2020 – Apr 9, 2021)
- **primary-document URL**: https://www.fda.gov/safety/recalls-market-withdrawals-safety-alerts/church-dwight-initiates-voluntary-recall-select-vitamins-due-isolated-manufacturing-issue

  contaminated with foreign materials, specifically pieces of metallic mesh
  material."* Per FDA/company summary: the recall followed "two consumer reports"
  identifying "the possible presence of a metallic mesh material" in lots made in
  a four-day window; the company stated it was "not aware of any reports of
  consumer illness or injury to-date," and warned that "in some severe cases,
  ingesting a metallic material could lead to damage of the digestive tract."
- **affected melatonin lots (quoted from Commissary notice, captured 2026-07-19):**

  - (also recalled: Fiber Well 90/220 ct, MultiVites 150 ct, SleepWell 250 ct)

**IMPORTANT SCOPE NOTE — for a human to verify before scoring:** the recalled

same UPC as THIS product** (the 3 mg White Tea & Peach sugar-free 140-count).

does not spell out flavor/"sugar free," and the recall was limited to three

UPC/size-level match, not a confirmed flavor-variant match, and confirm against
