## Assays / content analyses (→ source_assays)

**No published peer-reviewed assay names this exact SKU by name.** However, a
federal class-action complaint reports **independent university mass-spectrometry
testing of OLLY melatonin products** — see the quoted primary language in the
Regulatory record below (Murphy v. Olly). Because that testing is the load-bearing
content-accuracy evidence for this product, it is cross-referenced here but logged
under litigation (it is a complaint's allegation, not a peer-reviewed assay).

- **Complaint allegation (quoted verbatim, Murphy v. Olly complaint, Dkt. 1,
  https://www.classaction.org/media/murphy-et-al-v-olly-public-benefit-corporation.pdf,
  captured 2026-07-19):**
  > "a university mass-spectrometry laboratory tested multiple bottles, including
  > Plaintiffs' bottles. The amount of melatonin was 165% to 274% of the amount
  > claimed."
  The complaint names the OLLY melatonin line as including **"Olly Sleep
  (regular, Extra Strength, and Ultra Strength), Immunity Sleep, Muscle Recovery
  Sleep, and Kids Sleep"** — i.e., the regular Sleep 3 mg product (this SKU) is
  within the named line. The complaint does **not** publish a per-SKU percentage
  breakdown; the 165–274% range is stated across the tested bottles collectively.
  This is an **untested allegation**, recorded verbatim; the melatonin-content
  question for this exact SKU remains open.

### Gummy-category context (NOT this product — do not attribute to this SKU)
- **Cohen et al. 2023**, *JAMA* — PMID **37097362**. Content analysis of
  melatonin gummies found wide deviation of measured vs labeled melatonin (and
  CBD where present) across gummy products. Directly relevant **gummy-form**
  context because this SKU is a gummy, but the study does **not** name or test
  the OLLY Sleep gummy. Cited as category context only.
- **Erland & Saxena 2017**, *J Clin Sleep Med* — PMID **27855744** — cited
  *inside* the Murphy complaint as background (measured melatonin ranged −83% to
  +478% of label across Canadian brands; serotonin found in some). Category
  context; does not name this SKU.
