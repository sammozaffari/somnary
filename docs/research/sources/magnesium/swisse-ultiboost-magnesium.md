## Form & dose transparency (magnesium-specific)

- **Salt:** single salt, **magnesium citrate**. AU active-ingredient line quoted
  verbatim (Chemist Warehouse + swisse.com.au, captured 2026-07-19):
  > "Magnesium (from magnesium citrate) 150 mg"
- **Elemental vs compound disclosed:** Yes — the label states the **elemental**
  magnesium figure (150 mg) and names the source salt (citrate). It does **not**
  print the compound (magnesium citrate) milligram weight, only the elemental
  yield. No hidden multi-salt blend: this is a single-salt product, so there is
  no per-form split to disclose. The AU manufacturer page explicitly states
  **"Formulated without vitamin B6"** (swisse.com.au, verbatim, 2026-07-19),
  distinguishing it from the cancelled B6 sibling.
- **Per-serving elemental for the two dosing regimens:** 150 mg (1 tablet, general
  health) or 300 mg (2 tablets, muscle-cramp use). Stated on-label.
