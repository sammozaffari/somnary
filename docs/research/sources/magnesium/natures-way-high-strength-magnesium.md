## Form & dose transparency (magnesium-specific) — PER-FORM BREAKDOWN DISCLOSED

verbatim from the manufacturer page and corroborated on Chemist Warehouse and

- **Magnesium (as Heavy Magnesium Oxide 500mg) — 301.6 mg** elemental
- **Magnesium (as Phosphate Pentahydrate 80mg) — 16.5 mg** elemental
- **Magnesium (as Amino Acid Chelate 10mg) — 2 mg** elemental
- **Magnesium (as Magnesium Citrate 10mg) — 1.55 mg** elemental
- **Total magnesium — 321.65 mg elemental** (stated on-label as the sum)
- **Pyridoxine Hydrochloride (Vitamin B6) — see B6 status** (two figures circulate)
- **Colecalciferol (Vitamin D3 100IU) — 2.5 micrograms**
- **Manganese Amino Acid Chelate 40mg (Equiv. to Manganese 4mg)**

**Transparency reading (recorded, not scored):**

- **BUT the split reveals an oxide-dominant formula:** heavy magnesium oxide
  contributes **301.6 of 321.65 mg (~94%)** of the elemental magnesium; the
  three "better-absorbed" salts (chelate, citrate, phosphate) together contribute

## B6 status (CRITICAL AU AXIS) — CONTAINS VITAMIN B6; two label figures circulate

**This product CONTAINS vitamin B6 (pyridoxine).** Two different amounts appear
across primary listings, indicating a likely reformulation — BOTH are recorded:

- **Current brand + Chemist Warehouse listing (2026-07-21):** "Pyridoxine
  Hydrochloride (Vitamin B6) **10.95mg** (Equiv. to Pyridoxine **9mg**)" →
  **9 mg B6/day** at 1 tablet/day.
  (https://www.naturesway.com.au/nature-s-way-high-strength-magnesium ;
  https://www.chemistwarehouse.com.au/buy/82790/...)
- **Pharmacy Online + several other listings (2026-07-21):** "Pyridoxine
  Hydrochloride (Vitamin B6) **50mg** (Equiv. to Pyridoxine **41.1 mg**)" →
  **41.1 mg B6/day** at 1 tablet/day.
  (https://www.pharmacyonline.com.au/nature-s-way-high-strength-magnesium-tab-x-150)

**[VERIFY] — the single most important fact to confirm on a physical current
pack:** which figure is the *current* formula. The pattern (a lower-B6 SKU
alongside a legacy higher-B6 SKU) is consistent with the TGA B6 crackdown driving
a reformulation down from ~41 mg to 9 mg B6/day. The two ARTG IDs (185943 and
395398) may map to the two formulas.

**On-pack neuropathy warning:** The TGA mandates the warning ("Stop taking this
medication if you experience tingling, burning or numbness...") only for products
delivering **more than 50 mg B6/day**
(https://www.tga.gov.au/safety/safety-monitoring-and-information/safety-alerts/health-supplements-containing-vitamin-b6-can-cause-peripheral-neuropathy).
- The **9 mg/day** formula is well below the 50 mg threshold → no mandated warning.
- The **41.1 mg/day** formula is also below 50 mg/day → below the mandated-warning
  threshold, but far into the range the TGA flags as still capable of causing
  neuropathy (the TGA warns even lower doses can, especially when stacked with
  other B6 sources). No on-pack neuropathy warning was observed on any surveyed
  listing for either figure. **[VERIFY]** on the physical pack.

Recorded fact: unlike Ethical Nutrients (100 mg B6/day, mandated warning), this
product's B6 sits below the mandatory-warning line on both label figures, but it
**does contain B6** — placing it on the *contains-B6* side of the central AU axis.
