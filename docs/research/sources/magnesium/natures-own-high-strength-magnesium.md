## Form & dose transparency (magnesium-specific) — PER-FORM BREAKDOWN DISCLOSED

**This product discloses the per-form elemental magnesium breakdown.** Captured
verbatim from the manufacturer page and corroborated on Chemist Warehouse,
2026-07-21. Active ingredients **per 1 tablet:**

- **Heavy magnesium oxide 515.46 mg** — providing **magnesium 300 mg** elemental
- **Magnesium amino acid chelate 125 mg** — providing **magnesium 25 mg** elemental
- **Magnesium citrate nonahydrate 217.39 mg** — providing **magnesium 25 mg** elemental
- **Total magnesium — 350 mg elemental**

**Transparency reading (recorded, not scored):**
- **Compound weight AND elemental split stated for all three salts** (a clean
  disclosure: 515.46 mg oxide → 300 mg; 125 mg chelate → 25 mg; 217.39 mg citrate
  → 25 mg). This is the *opposite* of a hidden combined total — a transparency point.
- **BUT the split reveals an oxide-dominant formula:** heavy magnesium oxide
  provides **300 of 350 mg (~86%)**; the two "better-absorbed" salts (chelate +
  citrate) together provide 50 mg (~14%). The marketing headline "3 forms of
  magnesium, including easily absorbed magnesium citrate" is true by *presence*
  but the elemental mass is mostly oxide. Recorded as a form/bioavailability fact
  (remedy-grade territory), NOT a sourcing penalty — but relevant to the marketing
  read (the "easily absorbed" citrate carries only ~7% of the elemental dose).
