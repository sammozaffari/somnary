## Form & dose transparency (magnesium-specific)

- **Salts:** SIX magnesium salts (a multi-salt blend). **Per-form elemental
  breakdown is FULLY disclosed** — for every salt the label states both the
  compound weight AND the elemental magnesium equivalent. This is NOT a hidden
  combined-total blend. Active panel quoted verbatim (manufacturer FORMULATION
  block, https://carusosnaturalhealth.com.au/products/super-magnesium, and Chemist
  Warehouse, captured 2026-07-19):
  > "Each tablet contains:
  > Magnesium Phosphate Pentahydrate 508.23mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 105mg
  > Heavy Magnesium Oxide 259.11mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 150mg
  > Magnesium Orotate 156.25mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 10mg
  > Magnesium Citrate 123.76mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 20mg
  > Magnesium Aspartate Dihydrate 66.67mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 5mg
  > Magnesium Amino Acid Chelate 50mg — Equiv. to Magnesium 10mg
  > Total Magnesium 300mg"
- **Elemental vs compound disclosed:** Yes — BOTH stated per salt. Elemental
  total = 300 mg. Reading (recorded, not scored): despite six salts, **~85% of
  the elemental magnesium (255 of 300 mg) comes from just two forms — phosphate
  pentahydrate (105 mg) and heavy magnesium oxide (150 mg)**; the four "premium"
  forms (orotate/citrate/aspartate/amino-acid-chelate) together contribute only
  45 mg elemental. This is a factual read of the disclosed panel, not a judgment.
- **Magnesium oxide note:** heavy magnesium oxide is present and is the single
  largest elemental contributor (150 mg). Per README/watchlist scope, **oxide is
  a FORM/bioavailability fact, not a watchlist additive** — recorded here, not
  flagged.
- **Vitamin B6:** **NONE.** No pyridoxine / pyridoxal / pyridoxamine in the ARTG
  ingredient list (298635) or on the label. This product is magnesium-only. See
  the Vitamin B6 section under Regulatory record for why this matters in AU.
