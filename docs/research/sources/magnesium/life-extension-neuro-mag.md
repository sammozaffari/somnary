studies behind "well-studied" test the **branded ingredient Magtein®**, funded
by the ingredient maker — **NOT** the Life Extension finished product:
- **Magtein cognition RCT (2022), PMID 36558392** —
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9786204/ (DOI 10.3390/nu14245235):

  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12832366/ (DOI 10.3389/fnut.2025.1729164):
  100 adults, 2 g/day Magtein (delivering ~145 mg elemental Mg), 6 weeks,
  double-blind placebo-controlled; **funded by Threotech Inc. (Magtein
  manufacturer), which provided the investigational product.** Does **not** name
  any Life Extension finished product.
- **Recorded for a human reviewer:** the Magtein trials are on the **branded
  raw material** (and one used a multi-ingredient Magtein-PS formula, not the
