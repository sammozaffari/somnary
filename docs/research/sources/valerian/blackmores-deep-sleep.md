**Transparency reading (recorded, not scored):**
- **Valerenic acid: NOT DISCLOSED.** No "standardised to valerenic acid" figure
  (neither a % nor an absolute mg) appears on the Chemist Warehouse listing or
  the Blackmores page (2026-07-20). This is the salient finding: **unlike
  Blackmores Valerian Forte (single-herb) which prints "valerenic acids 3.2 mg"
  per tablet, this COMBO discloses no valerenic-acid content for its valerian.**
  The strongest standardisation signal per the README is therefore ABSENT here.
- **Per-herb doses ARE disclosed (not a proprietary blend).** Each of the four
  actives carries its own quantified extract mg + herb equivalent. The valerian
  dose is NOT "buried" in a single undisclosed blend weight — it is stated (300 mg
  extract ≡ 1.2 g dry root/tablet). So the valerian dose IS visible; what is
  missing is the *valerenic-acid standardisation* of that valerian.
- **Extract-to-herb ratios disclosed** for each: valerian **4:1** (300 mg ≡ 1.2 g
  root); ziziphus **20:1** (250 mg ≡ 5 g seed); hops **5:1** (40 mg ≡ 200 mg
  fruit). Magnesium given as the salt (magnesium phosphate pentahydrate 363 mg)
  with elemental magnesium (75 mg/tablet ⇒ 150 mg elemental per 2-tablet dose).
- **Per-tablet vs per-dose:** doses above are PER TABLET; the direction is 2
  tablets, so the delivered daily valerian is 600 mg extract ≡ 2.4 g dry root, and
  daily elemental magnesium is 150 mg. A human comparing this to one-a-day
  single-herb valerians must double the per-tablet figures.
- **Species named:** *Valeriana officinalis*, *Ziziphus jujuba var. spinosa*,
