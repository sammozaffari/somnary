## Liver-safety relevance (ashwagandha-specific — root-only)

Recorded as an **ingredient-class safety context** for the ashwagandha component,
NOT an action against this product. A page-level ashwagandha safety note will be
needed (the analogue of valerian's liver advisory).

- **TGA safety advisory — "Withania somnifera (Ashwagandha) and very rare risk of
  liver injury" (published Feb 2024).** Primary TGA pages
  (https://www.tga.gov.au/news/safety-updates/withania-somnifera-ashwagandha-and-very-rare-risk-liver-injury
  and the safety-alert page under /safety/safety-monitoring-and-information/)
  **timed out on automated fetch this pass**; the following is corroborated via
  nutraingredients.com's report of the advisory (2026-02-26 reporting the TGA
  advisory) and search summaries — **route through the citation-auditor against the
  live primary TGA pages before publication.** Reported TGA wording:
  > "there may be a very rare risk of liver injury from taking Withania somnifera"
  - **Adverse-event reports:** the TGA received **12 reports** of liver problems up
    to **5 February 2024**; **seven** had enough information to suggest a liver
    injury that may have been caused by Withania somnifera. **Four cases required
    hospitalisation** (liver); the advisory also notes more common **severe
    gastrointestinal** symptoms, with additional hospitalisations for GI.
  - **Since the advisory:** search summaries report **4 further** adverse-event
    reports suggestive of possible liver injury; in 2 of those other liver-linked
    ingredients may have contributed; all resolved on discontinuation, none needed
    hospitalisation. **Confirm against the live TGA update page.**
  - **Label warnings:** **NOT currently required.** Reported TGA wording: "If
    further substantiating evidence arises, regulatory actions will be considered.
    This could include warning statements on product labels or other changes."
  - **Root vs leaf:** the TGA is weighing root vs leaf; **India recently banned
    W. somnifera LEAF use while permitting ROOT use subject to prescribed limits.**
    **This product is ROOT-ONLY (KSM-66®)** — on the permitted side of that split,
    which is the safety-relevant reason `_universe.md` wants root-vs-leaf captured
    on every card. Recorded as a fact (root-only), not scored.
- **International corroboration (cite at publication):** Björnsson et al.,
  *Liver International* 2020, **PMID 31991029** (DILIN 5-case cholestatic/mixed
  liver-injury series); the NIH **LiverTox** ashwagandha monograph; 2023
  Iceland/Denmark consumer warnings. (Not re-verified line-by-line this pass —
  citation-auditor to confirm each supports the claim as written.)
- **Pregnancy/breastfeeding:** standard reference position is **avoid in pregnancy

- **On this product:** **no on-label liver warning was observed** on the surveyed
