`_universe.md`): the TGA advisory **"Withania somnifera (Ashwagandha) and very rare
risk of liver injury" (Feb 2024)** warns of rare hepatotoxicity, and the TGA is
weighing **root vs leaf** (India permits root with limits; leaf use restricted) —

  side of the TGA's root-vs-leaf question. Recorded as a factual composition point,
