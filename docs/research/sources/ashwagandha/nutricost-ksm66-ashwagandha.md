## Liver-safety relevance (→ page-level safety note; NOT a rubric dimension)

> This section exists because ashwagandha carries a **page-level liver-safety note** (the
> ashwagandha analogue of valerian's safety note). Recorded as factual context for the safety
> module; **not scored.**

**1. Plant part — ROOT-only is the LOWER-scrutiny form.**
- The **TGA safety advisory "Withania somnifera (Ashwagandha) and very rare risk of liver
  injury" (Feb 2024)** warns of rare hepatotoxicity (and more commonly severe GI symptoms),
  with some AU cases requiring hospitalisation, and the TGA is explicitly **weighing root vs
  leaf** — **India permits root use and has restricted leaf use.** Corroborated by **Björnsson
  et al., *Liver International* 2020 (PMID 31991029)** and the NIH LiverTox monograph.
- Nutricost KSM-66 is **root-only** — the **lower-scrutiny** form (unlike the root+leaf
  products Thorne/Shoden, Green Nutritionals, NOW). So only the SECOND amplifier below applies
  to this SKU, not the root+leaf one.

**2. Import with NO AUST L — NO mandated AU liver warning reaches the consumer.**
- Because this is a **personal-import supplement with no AUST L**, it sits **outside TGA
  medicine oversight** and carries none of the AU labelling the TGA advisory may attach to
  listed ashwagandha medicines. A consumer buying via iHerb AU / Amazon AU gets the **US
  label**, which prints no AU liver-warning statement.

**Additional interaction note (recorded, not scored):** the **BioPerine/piperine** co-
ingredient is an absorption enhancer that can affect the metabolism of some medications — a
potential drug-interaction consideration to route to the clinician boundary on the remedy page.

**Net (recorded, not scored):** root-only (lower-scrutiny) **but** import-with-no-AU-warning
— so **one** amplifier (the import/no-warning one) applies here, plus the piperine interaction
note; **not** the double liver-flag that stacks on Thorne/NOW. The safety module for the
ashwagandha remedy page (avoid-in-pregnancy/breastfeeding per NCCIH; rare hepatotoxicity per
TGA/LiverTox; root-vs-leaf caveat) should reflect the root-only, import status of this SKU.
**Route the TGA advisory and Björnsson (PMID 31991029) through the citation-auditor against
live primary pages before publication** (TGA pages time out / 403 on automated fetch).
