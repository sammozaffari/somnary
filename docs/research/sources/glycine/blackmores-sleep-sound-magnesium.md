- **Per-active doses ARE disclosed (not a proprietary blend).** All five actives
  carry their own quantified amount; the glycine dose is stated (3.1 g), not buried

- **No glycine purity/grade claim.** No "pharmaceutical grade," "USP," or
  third-party-grade claim for the glycine was located (2026-07-20). The product is
  positioned around **magnesium bioavailability** ("MagAbsorb+", "high strength /
  rapid absorption magnesium"), not glycine purity. Recorded as ABSENCE of a
